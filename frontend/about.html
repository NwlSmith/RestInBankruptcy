The on-going pandemic has far reaching effects. By September 2020, bankruptcies in specifically the NYC region had already surged 40%. What does almost two years into a global pandemic look like for businesses in NY? What does it look like for businesses all across America? We aim to utilize visual representations to express the impact these unprecedented times have on businesses all over the United States.
